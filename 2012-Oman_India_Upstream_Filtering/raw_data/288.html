Ever since the expressway started dotting the countryside, car journey has become a big bore. No stopping in between because no restaurants, no resting places. However, Satish Seth who worships his Adag (Amma daddy as gods) parents and friends with 2g (2 gorilla) Gautaman, Scam Sam, Cbi (Chennai based intelligent) Surendra Hari and Swan alias Swana found this nice little restaurant okay to his standards.
