EMATIC EGLIDE PRISM TABLET RUNS ANDROID 4.0 [ICS], COSTS $157

Saturday, April 14

Yet another contender for the Micromax Funbook tablet is here. This time, it is a 7inch tablet running Android 4.0 aka ICS [Ice Cream Sandwich] from the Ematic, called the EMATIC EGLIDE PRISM TABLET.

The Ematic Eglide Prism tablet has its hardware specifications pretty much much similar to that of Micromax Funbook tablet, featuring a 7inch capacitive touch screen [Know the difference between Capacitive and Resistive touch screens here] display, 512 MB RAM and throttled by a processor of 1GHz ARM CORTEX A8 CPU. Both Ematic Eglide Prism Tablet and the Micromax Funbook sports the Google Android's latest Tablet OS offering - Android Ice Cream Sandwich [ICS]

The winning side of Ematic Eglide Prism Tablet as compared to that of Micromax Funbook includes:
The 3D experience from the display while using the Ematic Eglide Tablet with the provided 3D glasses
The inbuilt Flash memory storage capacity of 8GB in this Ematic Eglide Tablet as compared to that of Micromax Funbook, which has only 4GB inbuilt Flash memory.

However, the downside of the Ematic Eglide Prism Tab is that it does not support the official Android Google Play Store while the Micromax Funbook tablet does. Also, the  Micromax Funbook tablet features some good edutainment content due to their tie up with good content providers while the Ematic Eglide Prism Tablet fails to prove much sport when it comes to the content segment.

In terms of pricing, Micromax Funbook Android tablet has some advantage in that, the Micromax Funbook Android tablet costs you INR.6499

Ematic Eglide Prism Tab on the other hand, costs your $157 or approximately INR 8050.
