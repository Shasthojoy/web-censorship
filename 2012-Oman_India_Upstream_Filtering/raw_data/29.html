Welcome to the website of Rahul Yadav

Welcome to my website. This site has been designed around the theme of fall (Autumn). The image at the top simbolizes open windows which lets nature touch you, just an exploration of how a top image with slightly exceeded boundaries "opens" the whole page. Usually, I'm a big fan of simplicity, if I could, I would just put black letters on a white background. Feel free to browse around and find out more about me.

This website use's javascript due to xhtml standards not allowing the _blank tag (used to display sites in new windows) so in order to view the links on this site in a new window you may need to enable javascript in your browser. If you wish to find out how to do this, click here.
