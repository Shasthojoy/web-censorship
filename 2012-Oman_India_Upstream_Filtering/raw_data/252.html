Satish Seth recounts his travels to his Adag parents' village

Satish Seth finds travelling to his Adag (Amma daddy as gods) parents' native village an illuminating affair. Every time he visits his Adag parents, he learns something new. Often times he visits them with his friends 2g (2 gorilla sized) Surendra Hari, Scam Sam, Cbi (confused beyond imagination) Gautaman and Swan alias Swana. He loves to play with their two dogs named Lucky and Tommy. There are natural scenes to click. Cool mountain air to breathe in. All in all, Satish Seth finds peace at his Adag home. He recounts his travels here to share with his readers.

Satish Seth finds strange symbols at his Adag village

Indian symbols

On a stroll around his Adag (Amma daddy as gods) parents' village, Satish Sethi found many interesting phenomenon to explore about. On one such occasion he came across this lemon garland wrapped around the gate pillar. Why was it there? Was it thrown over that it came be there? Or was it purposefully laid out? He wondered. His friends 2g (2 gorilla sized) Surendra Hari, Scam Sam, Cbi (confused beyond imagination) Gautaman and Swan alias Swana also wondered the same.
